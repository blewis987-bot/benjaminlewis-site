How I work

Principles

  • Clarity over cleverness: if it cannot be explained simply, it is not ready.
  • Stable systems over quick wins: I optimize for repeatability and low maintenance.
  • Trust-first delivery: clean scope, clear assumptions, versioned outputs.
  • No pressure: calm collaboration, honest constraints, and direct communication.

What collaboration looks like

  • We start with a short exchange to confirm fit and define the outcome.
  • I propose a simple plan, a scope boundary, and what “done” means.
  • You get a usable deliverable, plus notes so you can maintain it without me.

What I do not do

  • Vague strategy without a concrete next step.
  • Rushed timelines that create fragile systems.
  • Automation that removes human judgment from important decisions.
